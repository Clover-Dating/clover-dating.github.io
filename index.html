The year is 2026 and the online dating scene is in shambles. The most popular apps are highly extractive, and anyone who uses them is certainly "braving it out there." The design of most dating apps is frankly insulting, given their brazen disrespect for users' time, attention, effort, and preferences.

So we're doing something about it. Clover is a labor of love built for the true benefit of its users. Here's what that means:

  • Deep compatibility matching through a weighted question system
  • No ads, no gamification, no swiping, no premium paywalls gating basic features, no dark patterns or addictive mechanics
  • Profiles designed to reward depth and personality
  • Funded by donations, not investors

We believe that technology has unbelievable potential to help people find each other, and that the current state of the most popular dating apps—especially the swiping paradigm—reflects a gross misuse of technology. In that spirit, our aim is to find you your best match, period.

We're funding Clover through donations with the goal of keeping it free and open. Right now we're aiming to raise enough money to cover development, marketing, and our first year of operations. If donation funding isn't sustainable long-term, we'll introduce a minimal subscription ($3–5/month) with ways to earn free access. Either way: no VCs, no investors, no compromises.

Every donation brings us closer to realizing the vision. Thank you for making this possible!
